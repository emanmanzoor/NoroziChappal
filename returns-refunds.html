Returns and Refunds

Introduction

At our shoe store, we want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. If you're not completely happy with your order, you can return the product within the conditions outlined below.

Return Policy

We offer a 30-day return window from the date of purchase for unworn shoes in their original packaging. To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • The shoes must be unworn and in the same condition that you received them.
  • The shoes must be returned in the original packaging, including all accessories and tags.
  • A valid proof of purchase must be provided (order number, receipt, etc.).

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, contact us at returns@shikaristore.com with your order number and reason for the return. Our customer service team will guide you through the process and provide you with a return shipping label if your return is approved.

Refund Policy

Once we receive your returned item, it will be inspected for eligibility. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days.

Please note that the original shipping fees are non-refundable. If your return is rejected, we will notify you via email with an explanation.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange a product for a different size or style, please contact our customer service team. Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the requested item is not available, we will issue a refund instead.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned, including:

  • Final sale or clearance items.
  • Gift cards.
  • Custom or personalized products.

Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please notify us within 7 days of receiving your order. We will arrange for a replacement or refund after verifying the issue.

Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding returns or refunds, please contact us at support@shikaristore.com.
